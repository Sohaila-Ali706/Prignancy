Non Pregnant / Prevention

non pregnant:

Stress Urinary Incontinence (SUI) can happen to anyone, even if you’ve never been pregnant. It occurs when pressure on the bladder (from activities like laughing, coughing, or lifting) causes unintentional urine leakage.

Management

Description: Regular Kegel exercises to maintain pelvic floor strength.
Duration & Repetitions : Hold contraction of the pelvic floor muscles (as if stopping the flow of urine) for 5 seconds, relax for 5 seconds; 10 repetitions, three times daily.
Setting: Home .
