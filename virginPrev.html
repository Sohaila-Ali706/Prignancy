Virgin / Prevention

Virgin:

Even if you’ve never had vaginal childbirth, taking care of your pelvic floor muscles is important for preventing Stress Urinary Incontinence (SUI). These muscles play a key role in bladder control, and keeping them strong helps prevent leakage during everyday activities.You're supporting overall bladder health and reducing the risk of incontinence in the future.

Management

Description: Guided Kegel exercises focusing on external cues without internal devices.
Duration & Repetitions : Hold contraction for 3 seconds, relax for 3 seconds; 10 repetitions, twice daily.
Setting: Home .
